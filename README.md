# Apple-Retail-Warranty-Analytics-Dashboard


## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Apple retail operations and warranty performance. It provides insights into product performance, sales trends, warranty claims, and geographical distribution of stores.

The goal of this project is to transform raw data into meaningful business insights that support better decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze product performance and pricing strategy
- Understand sales trends and revenue drivers
- Identify high-risk products through warranty claims
- Evaluate store distribution across regions
- Provide actionable business insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI** → Data Visualization & Dashboarding
- **DAX** → Measures & Calculations
- **Power Query** → Data Cleaning & Transformation

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset consists of multiple related tables:
- **Category** → Product categories
- **Products** → Product details (price, launch date)
- **Sales** → Sales transactions (quantity, revenue)
- **Warranty** → Warranty claims data
- **Stores** → Store locations (city, country)

1. How many total products are there?  
2. How many categories exist?  
3. What is the total number of stores?

5. What are the top 5 most expensive products?  
6. What is the total sales generated?  
7. Which product sells the most?  
8. Which category has the most warranty claims?  
9. What percentage of claims are rejected?  
10. Which products have the highest failure rate?  
11. How do warranty claims change over time?  
12. Which year had the highest product launches?

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- 📊 Sales show a consistent upward trend, indicating business growth.
- ⚠️ Laptop category (especially MacBook products) has the highest warranty claims, indicating potential reliability concerns.
- 💰 High-priced products contribute significantly to revenue, while lower-priced items drive volume.
- 🌍 The United States and major cities like Singapore and Tokyo dominate store presence.
- 📉 Uneven store distribution highlights opportunities for expansion in underrepresented regions.
